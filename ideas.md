## Design Approach 1: Luxury Minimalism with Warm Gold Accents

**Design Movement:** Modern Luxury Minimalism with Art Deco influences

**Core Principles:**
1. Elegance through simplicity—clean white/cream backgrounds with strategic gold accents
2. Generous whitespace and breathing room between sections
3. Premium typography hierarchy using serif + sans-serif pairing
4. Trust-building through clarity and transparency

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Cream/Off-white (#F8F6F3) as the main background—evokes luxury, purity, and high-end retail
- Accent: Deep Gold (#D4AF37) for CTAs, highlights, and brand elements—represents wealth, trust, and craftsmanship
- Secondary: Warm Charcoal (#3A3A3A) for text and structural elements—sophisticated and readable
- Tertiary: Soft Beige (#E8E4DC) for subtle dividers and card backgrounds
- Emotional intent: Timeless elegance, trustworthiness, premium positioning

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric hero with text on left, large hero image on right (or full-width with text overlay)
- Vertical card-based sections with alternating layouts (image left/right)
- Grid-based product showcase (3-4 columns) with generous gaps
- Sticky navigation with minimal styling—only logo + LINE CTA visible

**Signature Elements:**
1. Gold vertical accent bars (2-4px width) on the left edge of key sections
2. Subtle gold underlines beneath headings and CTAs
3. Circular product image containers with soft shadows
4. Hand-drawn or custom serif font for main headline

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover effects: Subtle gold glow or scale (1.02x) on product cards
- CTAs have smooth color transitions from gold to deeper gold on hover
- Smooth scroll behavior with fade-in animations as sections enter viewport
- LINE button is always prominent with slight pulse animation

**Animation:**
- Fade-in on scroll (opacity 0 → 1 over 600ms)
- Subtle scale animations on hover (1 → 1.05)
- Smooth color transitions (300ms) on interactive elements
- Gold accent bars slide in from left on section load
- No excessive motion—keep it refined and professional

**Typography System:**
- Display Font: Playfair Display (serif) for headings—conveys luxury and tradition
- Body Font: Inter or Poppins (sans-serif) for body text—modern and readable
- Hierarchy: H1 (48px bold), H2 (36px bold), H3 (24px semibold), Body (16px regular)
- Letter spacing: Slightly increased (0.5-1px) on headings for elegance

## Design Decision

**Selected Approach: Design Approach 2 - Bold Red + Gold with Dynamic Energy**

This approach was chosen because:
1. **Strong Brand Alignment:** Red + Gold directly matches the Facebook page's visual identity (red background, gold text in logo)
2. **Cultural Resonance:** Red and gold are auspicious colors in Thai culture, reinforcing trust and premium positioning
3. **High Conversion Focus:** Bold CTAs and dynamic layouts naturally guide users toward LINE contact
4. **Visual Impact:** The diagonal cuts and layered sections create a memorable, modern aesthetic that stands out from generic gold shop websites
5. **Mobile-First:** The bold, simplified color scheme works exceptionally well on small screens

**Design Philosophy for This Project:**
- **Primary Colors:** Deep Red (#C41E3A) + Bright Gold (#FFD700)
- **Background:** Cream (#FFFBF0) for contrast and readability
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat) for headings, Open Sans for body
- **Interaction:** Energetic hover effects, pulsing CTAs, smooth transitions
- **Conversion Strategy:** LINE button is always visible and prominent with pulse animation
- **Trust Elements:** Transparency in pricing, clear contact information, authentic product images
